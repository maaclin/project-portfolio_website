About Me

My journey into DevOps started in the trenches of IT support at Bell Integration (supporting Blackrock) and SYDRC, where I discovered something hated by most: I actually enjoy when things break. There's something deeply satisfying about diving into a problem, understanding what went wrong and crafting a solution.

That's what drew me to DevOps – the perfect blend of problem-solving and prevention through automation. Why manually deploy something fifty times when you can automate it once and never worry about human error again? Currently working towards my AWS Solutions Architect Associate certification and actively building projects that showcase real-world DevOps scenarios. From service desk tickets to infrastructure as code – I'm ready to bring my troubleshooting mindset and growing technical toolkit to a DevOps role where I can help teams ship faster and sleep better.
